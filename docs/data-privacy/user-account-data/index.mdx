At Exlint, we take data privacy seriously. When it comes to user and account data, we want to ensure that our customers understand what information we collect and how it is used.

It's important to notice that if you are using the Exlint app, in order to use the service we need you to have an account with us.
This account includes personal data such as your email address and also the group you created on the app.
This information is necessary for us to provide you with access to the Exlint service and to communicate with you regarding your account and usage of the service.

We also collect usage data related to your account, such as the number of times you run Exlint and the results of those runs.
This data is used to improve the performance and functionality of the Exlint service.

We understand that the security and privacy of your personal and usage data is of the utmost importance, and we take great care to protect it.
We maintain strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

If you have any questions or concerns about our data privacy practices, please contact us at contact@exlint.io
